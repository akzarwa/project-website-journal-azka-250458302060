Day Of Summer

Summer, the warmest season, typically follows spring and precedes autumn. It's characterized by long, sunny days, high temperatures, and is a time when many people enjoy outdoor activities and vacations. The summer solstice, the day with the most daylight, marks the beginning of the season in each hemisphere.
