Coquette style is a hyper-feminine, romantic, and playful fashion aesthetic popularized by Gen Z on TikTok, characterized by elements like bows, lace, pearls, and pastels, drawing inspiration from historical periods such as the Rococo era and the 1950s. The name comes from the French word for a woman who flirts, and the style embodies a sweet, charming, and demure allure, often incorporating delicate fabrics, feminine silhouettes, and delicate accessories.
